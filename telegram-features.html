Telegram Key Features

End-to-End Encryption

Telegram prioritizes your privacy with end-to-end encryption in Secret Chats. This ensures that only the intended recipient can read your messages, making Telegram a secure choice for private conversations.

Cross-Platform Sync

With Telegram, you can stay connected across devices. Whether on Android, iOS, desktop, or web, your messages, contacts, and files are always synced and easily accessible.

File Sharing Without Limits

Share large files, high-quality photos, and videos with ease. Telegram allows file transfers up to 2GB per file, ideal for work or personal use.

Group Chats & Channels

Connect with communities and teams in Telegram’s large group chats, supporting up to 200,000 members. For one-way communication, channels allow you to broadcast messages to unlimited subscribers.

Voice and Video Calls

Stay close with friends and family through high-quality voice and video calls, no matter where they are. All calls are secured with end-to-end encryption.

Self-Destructing Messages

Telegram lets you set messages to self-destruct in Secret Chats. Once the recipient views the message, it automatically deletes itself for added privacy.

Customization and Themes

Personalize Telegram with custom themes, colors, and wallpapers. You can also add stickers to make your chats more fun and expressive.

Bot Integration

Telegram’s bot platform allows users to automate tasks like getting news updates, checking the weather, and even playing games. Developers can create custom bots for unique functionalities.

Cloud Storage

All of your chats, files, and media are stored securely in the cloud. This means you can access your entire Telegram history from any device without taking up space on your device.
