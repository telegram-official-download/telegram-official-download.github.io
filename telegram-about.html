About Telegram

Our Mission

Telegram aims to provide a secure, free, and globally accessible platform for messaging and community building. With end-to-end encryption and privacy controls, we strive to make private communication simple and accessible for everyone.

The Telegram Story

Founded in 2013 by brothers Nikolai and Pavel Durov, Telegram was created as an alternative to traditional messaging apps, focusing on security and user privacy. Since then, it has grown into a global messaging platform used by millions worldwide.

Why Choose Telegram?

With features like large group chats, file sharing, and customizable themes, Telegram is designed for both individual and community use. Our commitment to privacy and open-source software has set us apart as a reliable choice for secure communication.
